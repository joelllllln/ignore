Planet 1

How to play

  • Defenders auto-fire at dots. Dots are TANKY — more damage = faster kills = more income, so your defenders & their skill trees ARE your economy.
  • Armored dots (double-ringed) have huge defense and move slowly — they need real firepower but drop a big bounty, and their cash orbs are heavy & slow, so you'll want stronger collectors.
  • Every planet has its own native enemy race with a unique ability & look — splitters that split again and again, shielded bastions, cloakers, teleporting wraiths, loot-eating devourers, minion-spawning broods and more. The tougher a dot, the more spikes & rings it grows. Tap a planet on the map to see its native race.
  • Big kills drop heavy loot that a collector must sit and consume (watch the ring fill) — small dots are instant, fat ones tie a collector up.
  • Tap any defender (or hit ⬆ Tree) to open its skill tree — an interconnected node map. Allocate outward from the centre; each node needs a connected node first. Nodes stack additively (each adds a flat bonus that sums — linear, no runaway; early nodes feel strong, later ones taper), and ✦ keystones add multishot AND a crazy weapon specialization — ✦Explosive Rounds (bombs), ✦Chain Lightning, or ✦Piercing Laser. A whole ◈ Mind branch upgrades the unit's intelligence & coordination — smart units avoid overkill, don't both shoot the same doomed dot, and triage the most valuable targets, so no shot is wasted. Costs climb steeply, so plan your route. Every unit of that class shares the tree.
  • Collectors gather the cash dots drop. Buy more in the COLLECTORS tab and unlock new classes across all three systems: Drone → Drone Swarm → Heavy Collector → Magnet → Tractor → Black Hole → Wormhole (the last two slowly drag everything in). Each has its own skill web.
  • Conquer planets to unlock Travel and add each world to your idle empire (it keeps earning for you forever, even offline). You still rebuild from scratch on each planet. You steamroll within a solar system as each conquer bar eases down, then a new system spikes the difficulty back up and you climb again. A planet is a real hours-long campaign — active play (drawing, abilities, fat upgrades) is the fast path.
  • Every conquered planet keeps earning for you while you're away, and your whole idle empire grows stronger the more worlds you hold — early planets are an active grind, but lategame your empire can largely idle you to the next conquest, so you never have to hand-manage all 18.
  • ECONOMY tab boosts cash value, spawn rate, capacity & luck.
  • Tap the planet bar (top) for the star map — three solar systems of planets. Travel to new planets for tougher dots, more of them, and bigger payouts.
  • Draw across the field to blast dots yourself.
