How to play

  • 🔫 Defenders auto-fire at dots. Buy more (and new types) from the DEFENCE tab.
  • 🌳 Tap any defender to open its own upgrade tree (3 paths) — every unit grows, none go obsolete.
  • 🛰️ Drones collect the cash dots drop. One isn't enough — buy more in the DRONE tab; they split up to cover the field.
  • 💰 ECONOMY tab boosts cash value, spawn rate, capacity & luck.
  • 🌌 Tap the galaxy bar (top) for the map. Travel for tougher dots, more of them, and bigger payouts.
  • ✦ Reach Galaxy 10 to Rebirth for permanent Star Dust upgrades.
  • ✍️ Draw across the field to blast dots yourself.
