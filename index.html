How to play

  • 🔻 Dots fall from the top. Each has health that grows every wave.
  • 🔫 Your cannons auto-fire at the nearest dot. Kills earn ◆ coins.
  • 🛒 Spend coins on Damage, Fire Rate, +Cannon, Coin Boost — costs rise each level.
  • 🌊 Clear a wave's quota to advance. Every 10th wave is a BOSS.
  • 🛡️ Dots that reach the bottom hurt your Base. If it falls, you regroup a few waves back — never game over.
  • ⏩ Speed fast-forwards the assault (more coins, more pressure).
  • 👆 Tap a dot to blast it yourself.
