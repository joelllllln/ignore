Field Manual

  • 🌌 Star Map — zoom Universe → Galaxy → System. Tap a lit planet to invade.
  • 🧱 Build — pick a defender, tap a grid cell to deploy. Hold & drag to relocate.
  • 🎯 Tune — tap a defender for its skill tree & target priority (First/Close/Strong/Fast/Weak).
  • 🐝 The Hive — 7 species + a boss finale on every world. They crawl your Nexus & defenders.
  • ⚙ Efficiency — damage lowers efficiency, slowing conquest & income. Never game-over.
  • ⏩ Speed — higher speed = faster conquest & a reward multiplier. Fire rate stays fixed → more pressure.
  • 👆 Tap the sky above your grid to strike the swarm yourself.
  • ⬡ Tech — earn Cores by conquering; spend on permanent upgrades.
