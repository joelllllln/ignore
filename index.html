How to Play

  • 🌌 Star Map — zoom from the universe → galaxy → solar system. Tap a lit planet to invade it.
  • 🧱 Build grid — tap a defender in the palette, then tap a grid cell to deploy. Hold & drag a defender to move it.
  • 🎯 Targeting — tap a defender to open its panel: set target priority (First / Close / Strong / Fast / Weak) and spend energy on its skill tree.
  • 🐝 The hive — many enemy species with different speed, armor, abilities & animations crawl toward your defenders.
  • ⚙️ Efficiency — damaged defenders lower efficiency, slowing completion & income. No game over — just slower conquest.
  • ⏩ Speed — higher speed = faster progress & a reward multiplier, but the swarm moves faster (fire rate stays fixed → more pressure).
  • 👆 Tap the sky (above your grid) to strike enemies yourself.
