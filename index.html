How to play

  • 🔫 Defenders auto-fire at dots. Buy more (and new types) from the DEFENCE tab.
  • 🌳 Tap any defender (or hit ⬆ Tree) to open its skill web — a branching tree of paths, cross-links & keystones. Every unit of that class grows together.
  • 🛰️ Collectors gather the cash dots drop. Buy more in the COLLECTORS tab and unlock new classes: Drone → Drone Swarm → Black Hole (slowly drags everything in). Each has its own skill web.
  • 💰 ECONOMY tab boosts cash value, spawn rate, capacity & luck.
  • 🌌 Tap the galaxy bar (top) for the map. Travel for tougher dots, more of them, and bigger payouts.
  • ✦ Reach Galaxy 10 to Rebirth for permanent Star Dust upgrades.
  • ✍️ Draw across the field to blast dots yourself.
